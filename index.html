About I am Wantrepreneur

Iamwantrepreneur has taken an initiative to ignite Entrepreneurialism in the youth of our nation. We organize various workshops , conclaves and other events to promote entrepreneurship and build startup ecosystem at schools.

OUR VISION AND VALUES

This is an endeavor to initiate and develop the entrepreneurial spirit among the youth, the leaders of tomorrow. We also organize workshops, panel discussions, and training sessions on entrepreneurship and start-ups from time to time. In a nutshell, we prepare the youth for the transition from classrooms to workplace. All that we look for is the Enthusiasm and determination, the sparkle in your eyes and the irresistible surge of will and energy to execute your ideas.
